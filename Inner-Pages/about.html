What is SheTECH?
SheTech is a newly formed women-wing community of TechSias initiated by a group of students to provide a platform for women at tech to learn,share and experience new things and also to encourage them to enter the tech workforces.

Why SheTECH?
SheTECH's main aim is to inspire and create a passion for tech in girls by focusing on the idea of inclusive technological developments to make modern day innovations accessible to all women and conducting motivating events by women speakers. This communtiy not only enable members to upscale their technology skills but also provide them high visiblity to interact with like minded people with a shared focus and it is managed by the girls of SIAS. What is TinkerHub SIAS?
TinkerHub SIAS is a Campus Community Initiative of TinkerHub Foundation, a non profit organization registered on 2016. Fueled by the widening gap between technology employment opportunities and the availability of a local workforce to fill them is the major challenge we face today. Tinkerhub SIAS focus on enabling a peer to peer technology learning culture in campus. We aims at creating a potent workforce, capable of unlocking the vast reservoir of untapped potential, in the form of creativity and innovation that the youth of the 21st century holds.

TinkerHub SIAS Structure
The TinkerHub SIAS is Governed by mainly 4 membered core team including Campus Lead, Tech Lead, Creative Lead, Outreach Lead. This community is powered by TechSias, a tech community in our college, which is governed by students who are passionate in Tech.
